Matlab assignments:

  • The purpose of these assignments is to introduce you to Matlab and in particular to show how Matlab can solve multivariable calculus problems which would not be reasonable to do by hand.  I want to emphasize that this is an introduction only.  We will only be scratching the surface of Matlab and in particular you should not expect to ``learn Matlab" in this course.  Instead you will learn some commands which will tell matlab to do certain things.
